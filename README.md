

**House Market Analysis Dashboard – Power BI**
 **Project Overview**

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the housing market, helping users understand sales performance, price trends, and regional insights.
It enables data-driven decision-making for real-estate professionals, investors, and analysts by visualizing metrics such as unit sales, year-over-year growth, and price variations across different regions and sale types.

**Key Insights & Features**
**Overview Page**

Displays total units sold and sales value in the last 12 months.

Shows YoY sales growth by sale type (auction, regular, other, family).

Visualizes median sales price change by region to highlight regional performance.

Includes an offer price vs purchase price scatter plot showing the correlation between listing and buying prices.

**Sales Performance Page**

Sales by Region breakdown showing the top-performing areas (Zealand, Jutland, etc.).

Average Price per SQM by Region represented through a donut chart.

Offer to SQM ratio by sale type, giving clarity on property value differences by transaction type.

A detailed table showing sales by year, quarter, and month for time-series analysis.

Insights from the Key Influencers visual, identifying the factors influencing property prices.

**House Type Analysis Page (optional / if exists)**

Comparative analysis based on house types or property categories.

Highlights which property types perform better in terms of sales and pricing trends.

**Business Questions Answered**

Which regions have shown the highest growth or decline in median sales price?

What is the relationship between offer and purchase price?

How do sales types (auction, regular, family) impact year-over-year sales growth?

Which months and quarters contribute most to overall sales performance?

**Tools & Technologies**

Microsoft Power BI Desktop

Data Source: Housing Dataset (CSV/Excel)

Data Transformation: Power Query for cleaning and shaping data

DAX Measures: Used for calculating YoY growth, median sales price, and ratios

**KPIs Used**
Metric	Description
Units Sold	Total properties sold in the latest year/quarter
Total Sales (bn)	Sum of all property transactions
YoY Sales Growth (%)	Percentage growth in sales compared to previous year
Median Sales Price Change	Regional change in property price
Offer vs Purchase Ratio	Difference between listed and actual selling price
**Report Navigation**

Overview – High-level market summary

Sales Performance – Regional and sales-type breakdown

House Type Analysis – (Optional) Deep dive by property type
