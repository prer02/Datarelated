** Project Overview**

The House Market Analysis Dashboard provides a data-driven overview of the housing market performance across multiple regions.
It helps in identifying sales trends, pricing behavior, and growth patterns using dynamic visuals and KPIs.

Built using Power BI, this report leverages DAX calculations, Power Query transformations, and interactive visuals to deliver real-time market insights for real-estate professionals and analysts.

Key Features
 Overview Page

KPIs: Units Sold (Latest Year & Quarter) and Total Sales (Last 12 Months).

YoY Sales Growth by Sales Type.

Median Sales Price Change by Region.

Offer Price vs Purchase Price Correlation.

Business Insights

The dashboard answers key questions such as:

Which regions have experienced the most sales growth or decline?

How do offer prices compare with purchase prices?

Which sales types perform best YoY?

How do monthly and quarterly trends affect overall sales?

Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Power BI Desktop

Data Source: Housing Dataset (Excel/CSV)

Power Query: Used for cleaning, merging, and transforming raw data

DAX Measures: For calculating KPIs like YoY Growth, Median Price, Offer-to-SQM Ratios

Visualization Types: Bar Chart, Donut Chart, Scatter Plot, Line Chart, KPI Card

Key Metrics (KPIs)
KPI	Description
Units Sold	Total properties sold in the selected period
Total Sales (bn)	Overall sales volume for the last 12 months
YoY Sales Growth (%)	Annual change in total sales
Median Sales Price Change	Region-wise median price trend
Offer vs Purchase Ratio	Gap between offer price and actual purchase price
**How to Use This Dashboard**

Download the .pbix file from this repository.

Open it in Power BI Desktop.

Connect or refresh the data source if prompted.

Use filters (region, sale type, or year) to explore insights interactively.
